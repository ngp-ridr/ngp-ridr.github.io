The inaugural NGP RIDR workshop convened stakeholders from across the five-state Northern Great Plains region to identify shared priorities for drought resilience research and action.

Participants included ranchers, tribal land managers, federal and state agency staff, university researchers, and conservation practitioners. Discussions centered on drought monitoring tools, adaptive grazing management, and the role of traditional ecological knowledge in building landscape resilience.
